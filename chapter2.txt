What you held in your hand,
what you counted and carefully saved,
all this must go so you know
how desolate the landscape can be
between the regions of kindness.

Etymology. The origin of the name "Oreo" is unknown, 
but there are many hypotheses, including derivations
from the French word or, meaning "gold", or from the
Greek word ωραίο (oreo) meaning "nice" or "attractive".
